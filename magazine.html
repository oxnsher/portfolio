Magazine Design



This magazine spread was designed to attract readers to consider the Maldives as their next travel destination. The layout was carefully crafted using grids and guides to maintain clear structure and visual harmony throughout the page. To avoid overwhelming readers with dense blocks of text, I arranged images alongside the content to break up the layout and enhance readability. Each column is well-balanced, with text and visuals working together to guide the readerâ€™s eye naturally from one section to the next.

The use of vibrant and inviting imagery helps capture the essence of the Maldives experience and evoke a sense of wanderlust. These images are spaced between sections to ensure the text never feels crowded or monotonous. Consistent fonts and spacing were used to create a clean and polished look, while headings and subheadings are clearly differentiated to improve navigation and visual hierarchy. The color palette complements the tropical theme, enhancing the exotic and luxurious appeal of the destination.
