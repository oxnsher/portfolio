Horror Escape Room VR Game



Overview of the Game
Genre: Horror, Thriller
Description: During the war, a mental hospital hid cruel horrors. Your ancestor, a kind doctor, defied its brutal director and was tortured for it. To stop the abuse, they exposed the truth, leading to the hospital’s closure. Now, the vengeful director has trapped you inside. Friendly spirits, remembering your ancestor’s compassion, secretly help you with clues to escape. To survive, you must follow in your ancestor’s footsteps, solve puzzles, and uncover the director’s dark secrets.

Special Mechanics and Interactions:
- Teleportation Locomotion: Players navigate by clicking the floor.
- Environmental Hazards: Dark elements and creepy audio to increase tension.
- Interactive Puzzles: Use VR controllers to manipulate objects and solve puzzles.
