Money Managing App Design Prototype



This app (FinanaceWizard), was done by my team and I. Our app aims to help people learn to be more mindful of their spending habits, especially in light of the rise in online payments. Our muted analogous colour scheme allowed certain elements to stand out diminish others into the background. The colour scheme was also gentle on the eyes and did not have glaring colours. The analogous colour scheme which is filled with shades of greens, blues, and purples complement each other. The greens reminded us about the colour of money and the blues reflected the abundance of wealth The typface, "Gill Sans", a humanist sans serif with clear roots in handwritten letters, is incredibly legible and readable in text and display work and it has a contemporary feel thanks to its distinct, kind, and original characters.

Principles of Design: Emphasis, Contrast, Balance, Hierarchy
Color Scheme: Muted Analogous
Tools: Adobe XD
Module: UI/UX Design
