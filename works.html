Mobile Application and Website Design



Travel Web Design

This website was made for a travel company that offers personalized and luxurious travel packages online. The muted colour palette associates with elegance, subtlety, and exclusivity. The muted tones can also suggest a sense of richness and luxury. Using the bold and attention-grabbing font "Abril Fatface Italic" as heading text together with the modern and clean Raleway typeface for body text can produce a visual contrast that enhances the sense of luxury. When used sparingly and intentionally, the header typeface can generate a sense of importance and exclusivity, making important statements or branding elements stand out. The sans-serif font "Raleway" has a modern, minimalist design. Its geometric shapes and simplicity can produce a modern and elegant look that is frequently connected to luxury brands that value modernity and minimalism. This was done using Adobe XD.

MINDS App Design

MINDS is a non-profit organization in Singapore dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with intellectual disabilities. I created this app for MINDS to teach people with down syndrome how to accurately count and understand the value of various coins and bills. For people with Down syndrome, learning, practising, contemplating, or understanding money management skills is crucial since it enables them to become more independent and self-sufficient in handling their finances. I used the same colour scheme and typography as the MINDS website for my app as I felt that it would be more beneficial for my target audience to see familiar visual elements and the typography and colours used were also very suitable for my target audience as they are easy to read and also colourful to attract my users' attention. To make the app more user-friendly for the target users, I also used haptics and kinesthetics. This was done using Adobe XD.

Money Managing App Design

This app (FinanaceWizard), was done by my team and I in our UI/UX Design module. Our app aims to help people learn to be more mindful of their spending habits, especially in light of the rise in online payments. The app promises to give users useful resources, and tools to help them make wise financial decisions and improve their financial well-being. Our muted analogous colour scheme allowed certain elements to stand out diminish others into the background. The colour scheme was also gentle on the eyes and did not have glaring colours. The analogous colour scheme which is filled with shades of greens, blues, and purples complement each other. The greens reminded us about the colour of money and the blues reflected the abundance of wealth The typface, "Gill Sans", a humanist sans serif with clear roots in handwritten letters, is incredibly legible and readable in text and display work and it has a contemporary feel thanks to its distinct, kind, and original characters. This was done using Adobe XD.

Portfolio App Design

This mobile app was created to showcase my portfolio in a mobile form. It consists of my brand, brand story, skills, contacts and previous work. I used a pastel colour palette as they are frequently related to delicateness and softness. Pastel colours like gentle blues, greens, and lavender are frequently used to evoke a sense of calm and tranquillity. For my typography I used "Gill Sans Ultra Bold" and "Gill Sans MT". Combining these fonts create visual contrast and a clear typographic hierarchy. With its bold and heavy appearance, the Ultra Bold variant can be used for headlines, titles, or emphasized text, drawing attention and creating a strong visual impact. Gill Sans MT, on the other hand, has a cleaner and lighter design that can be used for body text, captions, or smaller text elements to ensure readability and balance. This was done using Adobe XD.
