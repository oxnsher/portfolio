Portfolio App Design Prototype



This mobile app was created to showcase my portfolio in a mobile form. It consists of my brand, brand story, skills, contacts and previous work. I used a pastel colour palette as they are frequently related to delicateness and softness. Pastel colours like gentle blues, greens, and lavender are frequently used to evoke a sense of calm and tranquillity. For my typography I used "Gill Sans Ultra Bold" and "Gill Sans MT". Combining these fonts create visual contrast and a clear typographic hierarchy. With its bold and heavy appearance, the Ultra Bold variant can be used for headlines, titles, or emphasized text, drawing attention and creating a strong visual impact. Gill Sans MT, on the other hand, has a cleaner and lighter design that can be used for body text, captions, or smaller text elements to ensure readability and balance.

Principles of Design: Emphasis, Contrast, Unity, Repetition
Colour Palette: Pastel
Tools: Adobe XD.
