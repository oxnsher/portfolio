SPCA Animal Cruelty Poster



This poster is a visual tool used to raise awareness about the issue of animal cruelty and to advocate for the protection and welfare of animals. It also encourages viewers to take action and stand against animal abuse. The green colour scheme associates with nature and animals. Green is often linked to the concept of renewal and growth. It symbolizes new beginnings for the animals, freshness, and the cyclical nature of life. The typography, "Gill Sans Ultra Bold", was used for the heading due to its bold and attention-grabbing nature and the typography, "Gill Sans MT", was used because of its its clean lines, simplicity, and humanist design.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator.
