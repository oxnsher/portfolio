About Me



Brand Story

Hello, I'm Shernice, a dedicated digital design and development student at Republic Polytechnic. Surprisingly, my journey into coding and design was never predestined. Initially uninterested in these fields, I found myself inexplicably drawn to the digital design and development course, leading me to embark on this educational journey. Although UI/UX design came naturally, coding proved to be a daunting challenge. At the outset, it felt like deciphering an alien language, with technical terms and concepts overwhelming me. However, as I persevered and delved deeper into the world of coding, I discovered my passion for it. Completing a functioning website became a source of immense satisfaction, fueling my determination to pursue a career in programming and design. I aspire to continuously refine my skills while contributing to the creation of innovative and captivating digital solutions.
